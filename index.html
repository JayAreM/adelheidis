Artist, Teacher, Designer

Hi there!

My name is Heidi, and I'm a self-taught artist that has a never-ending interest about everything artistic. My artistic journey has been a wild ride of self-discovery, ranging from pencil sketches to dabbling in the vibrant world of acrylics. I've enjoyed the freedom of creating my own path without any formal training, letting my creativity take the lead. Every brushstroke on my randomly mixed-media canvas narrates a different chapter in my artistic development. I can't wait to apply my varied skill set to the digital world and dive headfirst into the world of graphic design.
